Much like the ancient wisdom found in its philosophy, which emphasises the cyclical nature of life, India’s return to prominence seems not just possible, but inevitable. The wealth and high culture that once became ‘The curse of India’, and made it a target for exploitation, are now the very strengths that fuel its resurgence. After over a millennium of lost grandeur, the Lion is ready to rise once more and reclaim its position on the world stage—stronger, wiser, and more united than ever before.
